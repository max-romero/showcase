# FAQ

## 1. I'm a retail investor. Why should I subscribe?

As a retail investor, investing is probably not your core source of income. You may have a very time-consuming job and/or personal life. Getting a filtered, analyzed, ready-to-use list of candidates to invest your money — or signals about a good time to sell — is of high value. The power of AI is at your service.

---

## 2. Why is it not free?

As you may know, intensive processes and calculations run by AI models are not free (token usage). At this early stage, the goal is that early subscribers help cover the cost of processing. More subscribers means a lower subscription price.

---

## 3. Can I customize the report?

No. A fixed format is the key to a low-cost, high-value report.

---

## 4. Where are the colors, charts, and icons?

Colorless, discreet, and very lightweight — all intentional. The narrative of the analysis and the data are what should resonate with you, not a bold green or red color that could bias your investment judgment.
